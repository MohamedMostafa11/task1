The Electronic Forensic Evidence Department at the General Department
of Forensic Evidence and Criminology at Dubai Police dealt with 218
examination cases during the first four months of this year, 15% of
which focused on disputes through social media.
The Director of the Electronic Criminal Evidence Department, Lieutenant
Colonel Rashid Lootah, confirmed that Dubai Police has allocated a team
of experts and qualified to uncover evidence in cybercrime, indicating
that this type of crime is on the rise due to the rapid development of
technologies and the misuse of some.
